WRTTINGS ON BEAUTY, STYLE, TASTE

This is a collection of essays written about taste,style, and beauty in post-modern graphic design. The writings have sparked great debates and reactions among the design community. These notions are an integral part of any design practice; yet it seems to be the most controversial and sensetive topic in the design discourse
